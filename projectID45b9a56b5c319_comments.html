This project provides fast formatting feedback to F1 formatters, by way of diffs. Your careful work in F2 is appreciated.


The Medicinal Plants of the Philippines

By

T. H. Pardo de Tavera (Jerome Beers Thomas, Tr.)

Published: 1901.

Language: English.

To Proofreaders:

  • Follow normal proofreading guidelines.
  • Watch for lots of diacritical marks and review the guidelines for proofing them if you are not familiar with how to do it.
  • Higher resolution scans can be viewed at Google Books: http://books.google.com/books?id=oXISCO2ABd8C 
  • Most pages have a footer with something like "Digitized by Google" at the bottom.  Please remove these footers.
  • If removing the footer leaves a blank page, please insert the [Blank Page] tag.

To Formatters:

  • Follow normal formatting rules.

 

To the post-processor: An HTML edition of this is required. We don't yet have any set standards for HTML, although these are being developed. In the mean time, make sure that whatever you produce satisfied the following:

  • Please make sure that the HTML validates to at least XHTML 1.0 transitional.
  • Anchors should be made named 'pageXXX', enabling the reader to jump straight to a particular page.
  • Page numbers in the Table of Contents and/or Index should be linked to their respective page anchors.
  • Footnote references should be linked to their footnotes.
  • Higher resolution scans can be viewed at Google Books: http://books.google.com/books?id=oXISCO2ABd8C    
  • Illustrations should be incorporsated into the HTML version.
  • Edition and printing information should be retained.
